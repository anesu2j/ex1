Liverpool wins the 2025 EPL title

After a long-awaited triumph, Liverpool clinched the Premier League title in dominant fashion, showcasing their relentless pressing, tactical brilliance under Arne Slot, and a unified squad hungry for success. It marked their second league title in 5 years, sending waves of celebration through Anfield and around the world.

Trent leaves

Despite winning the Premier League, Trent Alexander-Arnold made the surprising decision to leave Liverpool. His departure marked the end of an era, as the homegrown star had been a key figure in the club's recent success and a fan favorite at Anfield.

Fear not, Wirtz is here

Following Trent Alexander-Arnold’s unexpected departure, Liverpool moved quickly to bring in Florian Wirtz as his replacement. The young German playmaker was signed to boost creativity from deep, signaling a tactical shift and a bold new direction for the team’s midfield and build-up play.
